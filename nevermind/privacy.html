Privacy Policy — Nevermind!

Last updated: April 2026

Nevermind! is a simple puzzle game. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

What we store

The game saves your best times locally on your device using your device's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device.

What we do not collect

  • No personal information
  • No location data
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Third-party services

The game does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children

Nevermind! does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
